How It Works

My book is divided across the working days in a quarter, so every account in the patch gets one focused 15-minute touch per quarter, guaranteed. Paired with AI-generated Monday account briefings, prep is already done — I just show up and execute. No account goes dark, no favorites get overworked.

Why It Matters

Forced Rotation is the mechanism behind the Patch Score. No account stays cold for more than a quarter — and the daily rhythm is what lifts the score, week over week.

The Ideal Daily Skill — What It Delivers

  • Smart account selection — pulls from Cool/Cold tier first, weighted toward stale strategic accounts that haven't been touched in 30+ days
  • Real-time research — scans the last 30 days of news, earnings, leadership moves, hiring signals, and funding rounds for the account
  • Business Why mapping — connects the moment to MX product fit (Connect / Analyze / Engage / Act) and the strongest use case
  • Persona & entry path — surfaces the right buyer and the warmest way in (cold exec, partner intro, LinkedIn, customer referral)
  • Warming play — drafts a specific, account-tailored outreach idea matched to the trigger and product fit
  • Hands-free prep — 2-min briefing in Notion + draft message ready in clipboard, so the 15-min touch is pure execution
